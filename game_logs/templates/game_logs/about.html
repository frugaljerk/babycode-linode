About BabyCode

BabyCode recreates memories in code.

We take your baby pictures and remix them into game characters. See your little ones traverse digital jungles, swim across bit ocean, adventure the world, and more!

Our mission is to build a gaming community where parents can watch their little ones in action. Forever. We want to help parents to catch the magic moments in games and revisit them whenever.

Subscribe and become part of our digital community. Receive regular game updates and more!
